Galaktioni

Great Georgian poet of the twentieth century

Here is the time line of Galaktioni's life:

  • 1891 - born in Chqvishi, Kutaisi
  • 1900 - 1910 - Studied seminaries in Kutaisi and Tbilisi
  • 1914 - published his very first book
  • 1919 - published his second book "Crâne aux fleurs artistiques" which made him a leader of Georgian poetry.
  • 1937 - his wife, Olga Okudzhava was arrested during the repressions.
  • 1944 - his wife, Olga died.
  • 1959 - he was placed in the hospital in Tbilisi, where he ended his life by jumping from the hospital window. he was buried at the Mtatsminda pantheon.

I deeply love the violet virgin Spread of snow away from the bridge, The sad wet coldness that I feel surging In patient love’s enduring spirit.

beggining part from one of the most famous poem of Galaktioni, "Snow".
